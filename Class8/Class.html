Ingredient list

  • 1/2 cup cocoa butter
  • 1/2 cup virgin coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup indian cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup agave syrup for sweetening
  • 1 cup of love

    • Instruction

  • Grate 1 cup of the cocoa butter. It will melt easier when grated. Measure also 1/2 cup of coconut oil.
  • Place cocoa butter and coconut oil in a water in a small, cup or bowl. Then place the cup or bowl in a shallow pan.
  • Measure 1 cup cocoa powder. If you like to add any other dry ingredients, measure them out now and stir them together with the cocoa powder.
